Services

I can help plan and book just about any kind of trip, whether you're starting from zero or already have a dream itinerary.

Cruise Planning

From Caribbean sun to Alaskan glaciers, I'll help you find the perfect sailing. With Royal Caribbean University training and firsthand cruising experience, I know the ships, the itineraries, and which upgrades are really worth it.

City Adventures

Immersive single or multi-stop trips built around how you want to travel and what you want to experience. I'll map out the sights, the neighborhoods, the restaurants, and the moments you'll talk about for years.

  • Activity & tour recommendations
  • Day-by-day planning

Everything Else

Beach vacations, all-inclusives, resort stays, friend and family trips, honeymoons, or solo adventures. If you're going somewhere, I can elevate your trip with exclusive perks and personalized service.

  • Resort & all-inclusive booking
  • Flights & transportation
  • Travel insurance guidance
